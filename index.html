Welcome to Radiant Royals! Whether you found us through Reddit or through one of our Shouts, we’re glad you came for a visit because we are prepared, engaging, and always striving to offer much more! Radiant Royals is composed of a mix of people who focus on Casual to Midcore styles of gameplay. From running Extreme Primal raids to simply running daily roulettes, we’re there and waiting. We are North America-based, and our members are most active during evening times.

Midcore Progression - AKA Extreme Primal fights - is what we're all about. These fights are intended for max level players, require teamwork, and mastery of one's job. For casuals interested in midcore, FC Raid Nights are held to revisit, learn, and clear old extreme primal fights. If you're ready for Zurvan Ex, you'll be a welcome addition to our FC Raiding Static whose members have already cleared it.

If Midcore Progression isn't your cup of tea, you'd be glad to know we run a variety casual content and let's face it, everyone needs time to sit back and relax! We hunt for treasures in Aquapolis, run 24 man raids for weekly tokens, daily roulettes, and ARR Extreme Primals for their ponies. Besides what the game has to offer, we hold monthly FC Lotteries and FC social events!
