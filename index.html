Welcome to Radiant Royals! Whether you found us through Reddit or through one of our Shouts, we’re glad you came for a visit because we are prepared, engaging, and always striving to offer much more! Radiant Royals is composed of a mix of people who focus on Casual to Midcore styles of gameplay. From running Extreme Primal raids to simply running daily roulettes, we’re there and waiting. We are North America-based, and our members are most active during evening times.

Midcore Progression

Casual Fun

Midcore Progression - AKA Extreme Primal fights - is what makes us different. These fights are challenging, but rewarding. We are currently working on Zurvan Extreme and once it has been conquered, we will continue learning & farming other Heavensward primals for loot.

If Midcore Progression isn't for you, you'd be glad to know we run a variety casual content and let's face it, everyone needs time to sit back and relax! We hunt for treasures in Aquapolis, run 24 man raids for weekly tokens, daily roulettes, and ARR Extreme Primals for their ponies!
