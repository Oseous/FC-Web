Welcome to Radiant Royals! Whether you found us through Reddit or through one of our Shouts, we’re glad you came for a visit because we are prepared, engaging, and always striving to offer much more! Radiant Royals is composed of a mix of people from social players to players who want to challenge hardcore content with casual attitude. From running Extreme Primal and Savage raids to simply running daily roulettes, we’re there and waiting. We are North America-based, and our members are most active during evening times.

Challenging PvE fights - this is what we're all about. These fights are intended for max level players, require teamwork, and mastery of one's job. If you want to get your feet wet, FC Raid Nights are held to revisit, learn, and clear older fights that can still prove to be difficult. If you want to be challenging the newest and hardest fights in the game, join the FC static!

If Raiding isn't your cup of tea, you'd be glad to know we run a variety casual content and let's face it, everyone needs time to sit back and relax! We hunt for treasures in Aquapolis, run 24 man raids for weekly tokens, daily roulettes, and Ex Primals for their ponies and birbs. Besides what the game has to offer, we hold creative and fun FC events!
